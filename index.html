TibiaLootSplit

How to use

  1. In your 'Party Hunt' analyser select 'Copy to Clipboard'
  2. Paste the values into the input field and click 'Submit'

Example result:

Zeffek to pay 187k to Kusnier (Bank transfer message: transfer 187399 to Kusnier)

Isudin to pay 169k to Kusnier (Bank transfer message: transfer 168746 to Kusnier)

Paedron to pay 115k to Arwa (Bank transfer message: transfer 114793 to Arwa)

Total profit: 273k~ which is: 55k~ for each player.
