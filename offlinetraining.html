This tool calculates the time it will take you to reach your target skill while offline training only.

The tool assumes 12h of offline training per day.

The tool assumes:
- Magic level training for mages (Druids & Sorcerers)
- Melee (Axe/Club/Sword) training for knights
- Distance training for paladins
